Pinacolada is a tool developed by 90N45 that looks for simple WLAN attacks and then informs you about them as quickly as possible. All this with the help of Hak5's WiFi Coconut, which allows Pinacolada to listen for threats on all 14 channels in the 2.4GHz range simultaneously.
